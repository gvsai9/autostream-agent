# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream
AutoStream is a SaaS product that provides automated video editing tools for content creators.
It helps YouTubers, Instagram creators, and other social media professionals edit videos faster using AI.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- Price: $29/month
- Videos: 10 videos per month
- Resolution: Up to 720p
- Support: Email support (business hours only)
- AI Features: Basic auto-cut and trim
- Best For: Hobbyist creators just getting started

### Pro Plan
- Price: $79/month
- Videos: Unlimited videos per month
- Resolution: Up to 4K
- Support: 24/7 priority support
- AI Features: AI captions, smart highlight detection, auto B-roll suggestions, noise removal
- Best For: Professional creators and brands

## Features

### AI Captions
- Automatically generates captions for your videos
- Supports 30+ languages
- Available only on Pro Plan

### Smart Editing
- Auto-cut removes silences and filler words
- Available on both Basic and Pro plans

### 4K Export
- Export videos in full 4K resolution
- Available only on Pro Plan

### Noise Removal
- AI-powered background noise removal
- Available only on Pro Plan

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
- Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase
- No refunds will be issued after 7 days from the billing date
- To request a refund, contact support within the 7-day window

### Support Policy
- 24/7 support is available ONLY on the Pro plan
- Basic plan users receive email support during business hours (9am–6pm EST)
- All users can access the Help Center and documentation at any time

### Cancellation Policy
- You can cancel your subscription at any time
- Access continues until the end of the current billing period
- No cancellation fees

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro?
Yes, you can upgrade at any time. The price difference will be prorated for the remaining billing period.

### Is there a free trial?
AutoStream offers a 7-day free trial for the Pro plan. No credit card required.

### What platforms does AutoStream support?
AutoStream works for creators on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

### Can I use AutoStream on mobile?
AutoStream is primarily a web-based tool. A mobile companion app is coming in Q3 2026.
